## 📌 Overview

UnityUplift is a centralized platform designed to simplify the scholarship process for students, NGOs, and donors. It tackles challenges like complex applications and lack of awareness by providing a seamless and user-friendly system.

Students can easily explore and apply for scholarships, track their progress in real time, and make informed decisions using built-in insights. NGOs can manage opportunities and applications efficiently, while donors can contribute transparently.

A key feature of UnityUplift is its intelligent prediction system that estimates scholarship approval chances, helping students better understand their opportunities.
